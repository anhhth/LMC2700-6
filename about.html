ABOUT ME.

Welcome to my canvas!
A computer can generate 16,777,216 colors, and the human eye can perceive over seven million different colors. Everyone is born a blank canvas, and each small experience adds a splash of color to that canvas. This portfolio presents a part of mine.
I am Anh Ho, a sophomore majoring in Computational Media at Georgia Institute of Technology. I come from the gorgeous country of Vietnam, here at Tech to explore the culture, the people and the media in a land all the way on the other side of the globe. I have always been fascinated by colors. I have a passion for graphic design as well as for coding. Before going to Tech, I taught myself how to do graphic design and how to code; being at Tech is an opportunity for me to gain a more solid foundation and to learn much more. My major, Computational Media, is like the best of both worlds for me. I intend to have Media and Interaction Design & Experimental Media as my threads in my major, as they align with my aspiration to pursue UI/UX design as my future career. I am excited to see what new colors I can add to my canvas during my time here at Georgia Tech and in America.
